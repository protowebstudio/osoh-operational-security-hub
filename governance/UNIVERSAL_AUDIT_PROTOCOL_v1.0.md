# UNIVERSAL AUDIT PROTOCOL
Version: v1.0
Status: Binding Meta-Standard

---

## 1. Audit Definition

An audit is a deterministic verification process
that evaluates compliance against defined invariants.

---

## 2. Audit Invariants

A-I1: All MUST-level constraints SHALL be verified.
A-I2: Audit results MUST be documented.
A-I3: Audit procedure SHALL be repeatable.
A-I4: Audit output MUST be reproducible.

---

## 3. Measurable Criteria

- Compliance matrix exists.
- Verification checklist exists.
- Evidence references recorded.
- Audit timestamp logged.

---

## 4. Enforcement Mechanisms

- Automated validation systems.
- Manual review where automation insufficient.
- Failure MUST block release.

---

## 5. Audit Triggers

Audit SHALL be triggered:
- On release candidate creation.
- On critical vulnerability discovery.
- On dependency major update.
- On governance change.

---

## 6. Structural Scope

This protocol defines audit structure only.
It SHALL NOT mandate specific tooling.

---
