# UNIVERSAL GOVERNANCE MODEL
Version: v1.0
Status: Binding Meta-Standard

---

## 1. Governance Hierarchy

Governance SHALL follow:

Meta-Standard
    ↓
Project Constitution
    ↓
Release Attestation

Lower layers SHALL NOT weaken higher-layer constraints.

---

## 2. Governance Invariants

G-I1: All structural mutation MUST require explicit authorization.
G-I2: Authority mapping SHALL be deterministic.
G-I3: Escalation logic MUST be finite and defined.
G-I4: Governance documents SHALL be version-controlled.

---

## 3. Measurable Criteria

- Defined change authorization mechanism.
- Role or authority matrix documented.
- Versioned governance artifacts.
- Explicit escalation procedure documented.

---

## 4. Enforcement Mechanisms

- Change approval workflow.
- Traceable version increments.
- Documented authority boundaries.

---

## 5. Audit Triggers

Audit SHALL occur:
- On governance modification.
- On violation of structural invariant.
- On major architectural change.

---

## 6. Structural Scope

This document defines governance structure only.
It SHALL NOT define operational tooling.

---

END OF DOCUMENT
